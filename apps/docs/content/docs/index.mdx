**TTS Arena** ranks text-to-speech models the only way that really matters — by
ear, in a blind head-to-head. You type a line, two anonymous models read it
back, and you pick the one that sounds more human. Every vote updates the
leaderboard.

## Why it exists

Speech synthesis has never had a good yardstick. Word error rate measures
intelligibility, not whether a voice sounds alive; mean opinion scores depend on
a handful of listeners in a lab. TTS Arena replaces both with open, large-scale
human preference: anyone can listen, compare, and vote, and the rankings that
fall out belong to everyone.

## Quick facts

- Sign in with Hugging Face to vote — accounts must be at least 30 days old.
- Prompts are English-only for now and capped at 1,000 characters.
- Models stay anonymous until you've voted; only then are A and B revealed.
- New models appear quickly and settle as votes accumulate.
